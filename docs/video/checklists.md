# Video Checklists

## Mid-week Rehearsal

### Start

1. Get keyboard and mouse from closet (labeled "video")
1. Turn on devices:
    1. Power strip under desk (cameras)
    1. Roland V-8HD
    1. Datavideo RMC-300A
    1. Video iMac
1. On the iMac
    1. Within the eCamm software, change the setting to UNLISTED (no date/unscheduled) and start stream when rehearsal begins.

### During

1. Rehearse/save camera angles as presets as needed.

### End

1. On the iMac, within the eCamm software, end and finish broadcast.
1. Turn off devices:
    1. Power strip under desk (cameras)
    1. Roland V-8HD
    1. Datavideo RMC-300A
    1. Video iMac
1. Return keyboard and mouse to closet.

## Sunday Morning

### Start

1. Get keyboard and mouse from closet (labeled "video")
1. Turn on devices:
    1. Lobby TV
    1. Power strip under desk (cameras)
    1. Roland V-8HD
    1. Datavideo RMC-300A
    1. Video iMac
    ::: tip
    If the Media iMac is already running the ProPresenter software prior to turning on the Roland V-8HD device, you may need to ask the Media person to restart the ProPresenter software after the Roland V-8HD boots up. Sometimes when ProPresenter is already running prior to the Roland booting up, the DSK and video input from the Media iMac does not work properly.
    :::
1. On the iMac
    1. In Google Chrome, go to the YouTube Studio dashboard and verify that the YouTube streaming link for today's date is PUBLIC.
    1. Within the eCamm software, choose today's scheduled stream and verify countdown clock is running.
1. (optional) Plug in camcorder on tripod and adjust angle for use during service.
1. Using printed service order
    1. Plan/save camera angles for effective service capture. Camera preset list is located on Video iMac in the "notes" application. Take note which worship team member is leading each song and where/when speakers are on stage.
    1. Note DSK on/off setting for showing lyrics, videos, and other presentation elements.
1. Confirm Media iMac is displaying properly on the "HDMI 8" input of the Roland V-8HD.
1. Confirm audio signal to stream with audio tech.

### Go Live

1. 8:55AM, start scheduled stream
1. Follow service order, and as needed:
    1. Change camera angles
    1. Switch to media input (HDMI 8)
    1. Toggle DSK

### End

1. On the iMac, within the eCamm software, end and finish broadcast.
1. Turn off devices:
    1. Lobby TV
    1. Power strip under desk (cameras)
    1. Roland V-8HD
    1. Datavideo RMC-300A
    1. Video iMac
1. Return keyboard and mouse to closet.
