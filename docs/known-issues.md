# Known Issues

## Troubleshooting quick fixes

- No projector signal: verify the scaler is on and set to the iMac input; check the Roland program out is feeding the scaler and that projectors are on the correct HDMI input.
- No audio to stream: confirm the sound board USB to the media iMac is connected and the boardâ€™s mix 10 feed is patched to the Roland; check the Roland audio meters.
- Cameras not responding: ensure the RMC-300A is powered and on the same network as the PTZ cameras; reseat the Ethernet to the camera controller.
- Confidence monitor blank: make sure the Roland is powered and outputting Program; confirm the scaler output to the confidence display is selected.
- Lighting not reacting: check the DMX adapter on the media iMac and that Lightkey has the right universe/addresses; try the wall panel preset.
