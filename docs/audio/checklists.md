# Audio Checklists

## Mid-week Prep

Review [the worship team in Planning Center](https://services.planningcenteronline.com/plans) as a guide to understand the layout and complexity of audio setup you will need to provide.

## Mid-week Rehearsal

The worship team will rely on you during their mid-week rehearsal. Come to the rehearsal to setup audio equipment (mics, guitar DI boxes, in-ear monitor system, etc). Communicate with the worship leader for any specific needs.

## Sunday Morning

### Timeline

- 7:30AM - [power up](#power-up)
- 7:40AM - [start rehearsal](#rehearsal)
- 8:35AM - [Media sound check](#media-sound-check)
- 8:40AM - pre-service music begins
- 8:57AM - countdown video begins
- 9:02AM - begin service
- 10:30AM - [power down](#power-down)

### Power Up

1. Turn on Dell PC
1. Turn on QSC speakers on stage
1. Turn on other equipment
1. Take Sure mics off of their chargers in the closet
1. Help worship team get their wireless packs from the closet
1. Setup mics on stage
1. Prep on-person mics for speakers

### Rehearsal

- Assist the worship team during their rehearsal
- Dial in the mix

### Touch base with speakers

- For anyone who is speaking during the service be sure they know which microphones they should use.
- Quick training to remind people not to turn off the handheld microphones.
- Ask everyone who wears a mic to do a brief sound check.

### Media sound check

Around 8:35AM the Media tech should ask you to do a sound check of their audio elements (pre-service music, bumpers, videos).

### Go Live
1. 8:40AM - pre-service music begins
1. 8:57AM - countdown video begins
1. During service - follow service order

### Power Down

1. Clear the stage of audio equipment that should be locked in the Media closet.
1. Process Audacity audio
1. Turn off audio equipment
