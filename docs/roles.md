# Roles

The tech desk at the rear of the church has three known roles:
- [Media](#media)
- [Video](#video)
- [Audio](#audio)

## Media

The person running media sits at the left seat at the desk.

This person is in control of
- [Media iMac](./media/media-imac.md)
- [Projectors](./equipment/projectors.md)
- [Lobby TV](./equipment/tvs.md#lobby-tvs)

This person is responsible for:
- Preparing presentations using ProPresenter software. 
- Running presentations for the worship center projectors, lobby TV, live stream.
- Pre-service walk-in music
- Lighting of the worship center

When this person is needed:
- Prior to Sunday mornings doing slide preparation. Often this person comes during the Thursday worship team rehearsal but not absolutely necessary.
- Sunday morning worship team rehearsal (7:30AM).
- Sunday morning worship service (9:00AM).
- Sunday school hour (10:30AM). This is occassionally needed for special gatherings where slide presentations are needed in the worship center.

## Video

The person running video sits at the center seat at the desk.

This person is in control of:
- [Roland V-8HD Video Switcher](./equipment/worship-center-video.md#roland-v-8hd-video-switcher)
- [Datavideo Controller for PTZ Cameras](./equipment/cameras.md)
- Video iMac

This person is responsible for:
- Starting and stopping the live stream
- Switching video inputs that appear on the live stream
- Controlling the PTZ (pan-tilt-zoom) cameras

When this person is needed:
- Sunday morning worship service (9:00AM).

## Audio

The person running audio sits at the right seat at the desk.

This person is in control of
- Dell PC
- Audio Board
- Audio Equipment

This person is responsible for:
- Setting up necessary audio equipment for the entire worship service
- Running the sound board during the the worship service
- Recording audio of the worship service

When this person is needed:
- Mid-week worship team rehearsal, typically Thursday night at 7:00PM.
- Sunday morning worship team rehearsal (7:30AM).
- Sunday morning worship service (9:00AM).
- Sunday school hour (10:30AM). This is occassionally needed for special gatherings where audio is needed in the worship center.
